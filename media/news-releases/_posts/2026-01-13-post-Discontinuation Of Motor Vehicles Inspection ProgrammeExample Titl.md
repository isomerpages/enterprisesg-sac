<p>Following a comprehensive review of our Motor Vehicles Inspection programme,
we would like to inform that SAC will withdraw the Motor Vehicles Inspection
programme with effect from 12 January 2026.</p>
<p>We would like to thank the industry stakeholders and volunteers for the
support given to SAC for this programme.</p>
<p>For other SAC accreditation programmes, please refer here (<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.sac-accreditation.gov.sg/services/accreditation-services/</a>)</p>
